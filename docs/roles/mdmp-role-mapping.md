# Cyber Roles Mapped to MDMP

This matrix shows how each cyber role contributes during MDMP.
Cyber Operations Planner leads integration; analysts provide inputs.

---

## MDMP Step 1 — Receipt of Mission
- Host Analyst: Provide initial host risk considerations
- Network Analyst: Identify known network constraints
- Cyber Ops Planner: Assess cyber implications, initial estimate

---

## MDMP Step 2 — Mission Analysis
- Host Analyst: Identify host vulnerabilities and observations
- Network Analyst: Assess network architecture and exposure
- Cyber Ops Planner: Lead cyber IPB, define cyber tasks and constraints

---

## MDMP Step 3 — COA Development
- Host Analyst: Assess host feasibility per COA
- Network Analyst: Assess network feasibility per COA
- Cyber Ops Planner: Integrate cyber effects into COAs

---

## MDMP Step 4 — COA Analysis (Wargaming)
- Host Analyst: Identify host-level friction points
- Network Analyst: Identify network-level risks
- Cyber Ops Planner: War-game cyber effects and risks

---

## MDMP Step 5 — COA Comparison
- Host Analyst: Provide comparative host risk inputs
- Network Analyst: Provide comparative network risk inputs
- Cyber Ops Planner: Recommend cyber-informed COA

---

## MDMP Step 6 — COA Approval
- Host Analyst: Stand by for clarification
- Network Analyst: Stand by for clarification
- Cyber Ops Planner: Support decision brief, risk articulation

---

## MDMP Step 7 — Orders Production
- Host Analyst: Validate host-related tasks
- Network Analyst: Validate network-related tasks
- Cyber Ops Planner: Draft cyber annexes and execution guidance
