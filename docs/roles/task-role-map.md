# Task-to-Role Responsibility Map

This map clarifies which role owns, supports, or is informed on common staff tasks.

---

## Planning & Orders

### MDMP Execution
- **Owner:** G3 Operations
- **Support:** All staff sections (running estimates)
- **Integrator:** G3 synchronizes all inputs

### OPORD Production
- **Owner:** G3 Operations (base order)
- **Support:** All staff sections (respective annexes)

### Task Organization
- **Owner:** G3 Operations
- **Informed:** All staff sections

---

## Intelligence & Threat

### Intelligence Preparation of the Battlefield (IPB)
- **Owner:** G2 Intelligence
- **Support:** ENCOORD (terrain), Cyber Ops Planner (cyber terrain), Network Analyst (network terrain)
- **Informed:** All staff sections

### Threat COA Development
- **Owner:** G2 Intelligence
- **Support:** Cyber Ops Planner (cyber threat COAs), Host Analyst, Network Analyst
- **Informed:** G3 Operations, FSCOORD

### Collection Management / PIR Tracking
- **Owner:** G2 Intelligence
- **Support:** All staff sections (nominate PIR/IR)

---

## Fires & Targeting

### Targeting (D3A / F3EAD)
- **Owner:** FSCOORD (Fires)
- **Support:** G2 Intelligence (detect), Cyber Ops Planner (cyber effects), G3 Operations (assess)
- **Informed:** All staff sections

### Fire Support Planning
- **Owner:** FSCOORD (Fires)
- **Support:** G3 Operations, G2 Intelligence
- **Informed:** ENCOORD (obstacle-fires integration)
