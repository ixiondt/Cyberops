# SKILL PROFILE — Cyber Operations Planner

## Role Purpose
The Cyber Operations Planner integrates cyberspace operations into Army and joint planning processes to support mission objectives, commander decision-making, and operational synchronization.

This role focuses on **planning, integration, and decision support**, not tactical analysis or tool execution.

---

## Core Skill Domains

### 1. Planning Doctrine & Processes
- Apply MDMP and Joint Planning concepts
- Integrate cyber considerations into all planning phases
- Support commander’s intent and decision-making

### 2. Cyber Operational Awareness
- Understand cyberspace as an operational domain
- Articulate cyber effects and limitations
- Relate cyber actions to operational objectives

### 3. Cyber Intelligence Integration
- Translate cyber threat intelligence into planning inputs
- Support cyber IPB and threat COA development
- Integrate intelligence into war-gaming and risk analysis

### 4. Risk & Vulnerability Assessment
- Identify cyber-related operational risks
- Articulate risk in mission terms
- Support mitigation and acceptance decisions

### 5. Synchronization & Integration
- Synchronize cyber with fires, maneuver, ISR, IO, and EW
- Support targeting and effects coordination
- Build execution and synchronization matrices

### 6. Authorities & Constraints Awareness
- Understand operational authorities and limitations
- Ensure plans align with policy and ROE
- Identify approval requirements and constraints early

### 7. Orders & Product Development
- Draft cyber-relevant planning products and annexes
- Produce clear recommendations for commanders
- Ensure doctrinal consistency and clarity

---

## Key Attributes
- Big-picture thinking
- Clear written and verbal communication
- Comfort advising commanders and staff
- Strong doctrinal grounding

---

## Out of Scope
- Tactical execution of cyber techniques
- Host or network analysis as a primary function
- Tool-specific implementation details
