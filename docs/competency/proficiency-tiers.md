# Cyber Role Proficiency Tiers

This framework defines expected progression for cyber personnel across experience levels.
Applies to analysts and planners alike.

---

## Tier 1 — Junior (Foundational)

Characteristics:
- Executes defined tasks with supervision
- Understands core concepts and terminology
- Produces basic reports and observations

Expectations:
- Follows established processes
- Identifies anomalies but escalates analysis
- Requires guidance for prioritization

---

## Tier 2 — Journeyman (Operational)

Characteristics:
- Works independently within role
- Correlates multiple data sources
- Understands mission context

Expectations:
- Produces actionable assessments
- Advises peers and supervisors
- Identifies gaps and recommends improvements

---

## Tier 3 — Senior (Expert)

Characteristics:
- Anticipates adversary behavior
- Shapes planning and decision-making
- Mentors others

Expectations:
- Drives analysis direction
- Integrates across roles and domains
- Advises commanders and senior staff
