2. **Mission Analysis** → ATP 2-01.3 (IPB), FM 2-0 (intelligence), ADP 2-0 (intel warfighting function), FM 3-0 (operational framework)

## QUICK CITATION FORMAT

When citing doctrine in responses, use:
- **ADP 5-0, Figure 3-1** (specific figure)
- **FM 6-0, Appendix D** (appendix)
- **FM 3-12, Section 3-4** (section)
- **ATP 2-01.3, Appendix B** (appendix)
- **JP 3-12, Paragraph 3.b.1** (paragraph)

Example: *"Per FM 6-0, Appendix D, Annex H (Signal) is the responsibility of the G6/S6 and includes PACE plans, network diagrams, and spectrum management procedures."*
