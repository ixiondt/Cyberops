## Role Purpose
The Host Analyst conducts analysis of endpoint and host-based systems to identify, assess, and report malicious or anomalous activity in support of defensive and offensive cyberspace operations.

This role focuses on **host behavior, artifacts, and system state**, not network traffic or operational planning.

---

## Core Skill Domains

### 1. Host-Based Analysis
- Analyze operating system behavior and events
- Identify anomalous processes, services, and scheduled activity
- Distinguish expected system behavior from adversary activity

### 2. Endpoint Telemetry & Logging
- Interpret host logs and endpoint telemetry
- Correlate multiple host indicators into a coherent assessment
- Identify gaps in host visibility and data collection

### 3. Malware & Artifact Recognition
- Recognize malicious artifacts and persistence mechanisms
- Assess malware behavior at a high level
- Support triage and escalation decisions

### 4. System Configuration & Baselines
- Understand secure baseline configurations
- Identify deviations from approved configurations
- Assess security control effectiveness at the host level

### 5. Threat Indicator Correlation
- Apply threat intelligence to host observations
- Correlate host artifacts with known adversary behaviors
- Support identification of adversary tactics and objectives

### 6. Defensive Cyber Support
- Support detection refinement and tuning
- Assist incident response and containment decisions
- Provide host-level evidence for operational reporting

### 7. Reporting & Documentation
- Produce clear, concise analytical write-ups
- Document timelines and findings
- Communicate host-level risk to leadership and planners

---

## Key Attributes
- Attention to detail
- Analytical discipline
- Methodical documentation
- Strong understanding of operating systems and endpoints

---

## Out of Scope
- Network traffic analysis as a primary function
- Cyber operational planning
- Tool or vendor-specific implementation techniques
